What is an Ausbildung?

Ausbildung is Germany’s vocational training system. It combines practical work in a company with learning at a vocational school.

  • Usually takes 2 to 3.5 years
  • Trainees usually receive a monthly salary
  • Training takes place in a company and at school
  • After completion, trainees receive a recognized qualification
  • Requirements depend on the profession, company and applicant profile

Frequently asked questions

Clear answers for applicants and companies.

For applicants

What is an Ausbildung?

It is a German vocational training program that combines practical work in a company with learning at a vocational school.

Can foreigners apply for Ausbildung in Germany?

Yes, international applicants may apply. Requirements depend on the profession, company, language level and personal situation.

Do I need German language skills?

Usually yes. Many companies and vocational schools require German language skills because training and daily work are often in German.

Can Azubifly guarantee a job or visa?

No. Azubifly supports the process, but jobs, visas and residence permits cannot be guaranteed.
